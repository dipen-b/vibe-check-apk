### 2. THE GAUNTLET 🎮 (Daily Mood Quests)

**Turn mental health into an engaging game.**

```
What you get:
├─ 3 daily quests (different each day)
├─ Morning check-in quest
├─ Gratitude/reflection quest
├─ Mood analysis quest
├─ Streak counter (🔥 get badges!)
├─ Vibe Gems currency (earn & spend)
└─ Global leaderboard (compete with others)

Daily Quest Examples:
├─ "How are you feeling right now?"
├─ "What's one thing you're grateful for?"
├─ "Describe your day in 3 sentences"
├─ "Rate your sleep quality"
├─ "What made you smile today?"
├─ "Any challenges today? How did you handle them?"

Streak System:
└─ Complete all 3 quests = 1 day ✅
   ├─ Day 1-2: Normal rewards
   ├─ Day 3-6: 1.5× bonus multiplier
   ├─ Day 7: 2× bonus + 50 gems + badge 🏆
   ├─ Day 14: 2.5× bonus + 100 gems + badge
   ├─ Day 30: Premium badge + 250 gems 👑
   └─ Miss 1 day = streak resets 😢

Vibe Gems:
├─ Earn: 5-15 gems per quest
├─ Spend: Cosmetics, badges, premium features
├─ Gift: Donate to mental health charities
└─ Reward ads: Watch 30-sec ad for +10 gems
```

**Leaderboard Rankings:**
```
Bronze (0-100 gems)           🥉
Silver (100-500 gems)         🥈
Gold (500-1,000 gems)         🥇
Platinum (1,000+ gems)        👑
Diamond (5,000+ gems)         💎
Legendary (10,000+ gems)      🔥
```
