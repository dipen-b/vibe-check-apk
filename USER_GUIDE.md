# VibeCheck User Guide 📱✨

Welcome to **VibeCheck** — Your personal mood tracking companion with a twist!

---

## 🎯 What is VibeCheck?

VibeCheck is a **mental health and mood tracking app** that helps you:
- 📊 Track your daily mood and emotions
- 🔥 Share anonymous mood snippets with a global community
- 🎮 Complete daily quests to build streaks and win rewards
- 👥 Connect with friends and support each other
- 🏆 Compete on global leaderboards
- 💎 Earn Vibe Gems through challenges

---

## 🚀 Getting Started (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Download & Install
```
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "VibeCheck"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Wait for installation (~30 seconds)
```

### Step 2: Create Your Account
```
1. Open VibeCheck
2. Tap "Welcome" → "Let's Begin"
3. Select your age range
4. Enter your username
5. Enter your phone number (will be verified)
6. Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your phone
7. Add your first and last name
8. (Optional) Upload a profile picture
```

**Done!** You're now ready to use VibeCheck! 🎉

## 📚 Main Features Explained

### 1️⃣ THE RESONANCE FEED 🔥

**What is it?**
A TikTok-style feed where you can share and discover anonymous mood snippets from people worldwide.

**How to Use:**

#### Share Your Mood
```
1. Tap the "Feed" tab (middle)
2. Tap the blue floating action button (+)
3. Select your current mood:
   😊 Happy
   😐 Neutral
   😔 Sad
   😡 Angry
   😴 Tired
   
4. Write a quick message (max 100 characters)
   Example: "Just finished my project! Feeling accomplished"
   
5. (Optional) Upload a photo (will be auto-resized to 500x500px)

6. Tap "Post"
```

Your post is now live! It's completely anonymous — only your mood emoji and message show.

#### Discover & Resonate
```
1. Scroll through the feed
2. See posts from other users worldwide
3. Tap the 🔥 "Resonate" button if you relate
4. The more resonates = the more visible the post
5. View location tags to see where posts are from
```

**Why Resonate?**
- 🔥 Show support for others' feelings
- 📍 Connect with people in your region
- 💬 See what others are experiencing
- 🌍 Feel less alone in your emotions

#### Upload Photos
```
Photos are:
✅ Auto-resized to 500x500 pixels
✅ Compressed to load fast
✅ Secure in our servers
✅ Completely optional
```

### 2️⃣ THE GAUNTLET 🎮

**What is it?**
A daily interactive mood quest arcade where you complete 3 challenges per day to build streaks and earn Vibe Gems.

**How to Use:**

#### Daily Quests
```
1. Tap the "Gauntlet" tab
2. You'll see 3 quests for today:

Quest 1: Morning Check-in
└─ "How are you feeling right now?"
└─ Reward: 💎 5 Vibe Gems

Quest 2: Mood Journal
└─ "Write about your day in 3 sentences"
└─ Reward: 💎 10 Vibe Gems

Quest 3: Gratitude Practice
└─ "Name 1 thing you're grateful for"
└─ Reward: 💎 15 Vibe Gems
```

#### Complete a Quest
```
1. Tap on any quest
2. Read the prompt
3. Provide your answer
4. Tap "Submit"
5. Congratulations! Quest completed! 🎉
```

#### Build Your Streak
```
Complete all 3 quests = 1 day streak 🔥

Streak Rewards:
├─ Day 1: 🔥 1x multiplier (base reward)
├─ Day 3: 🔥🔥 1.5x bonus
├─ Day 7: 🔥🔥🔥 2x bonus + 50 gems
├─ Day 14: 🔥🔥🔥🔥 2.5x bonus + 100 gems
└─ Day 30: 🏆 2x multiplier + 250 gems + badge!

Missing a day = streak resets 😢 (but you can restart)
```

#### Earn Vibe Gems 💎
```
Gems are currency in VibeCheck:

How to earn:
├─ Complete quests: +5-15 gems per quest
├─ Build streaks: Multiplier bonuses
├─ Reward ads: Watch 30-sec ad for +10 gems
└─ Special events: Seasonal challenges

How to spend:
├─ Avatar cosmetics: 50 gems
├─ Profile badges: 100 gems
├─ Premium features: 200-500 gems
└─ Donate to mental health charities: Any amount
```

#### Global Leaderboard 🏆
```
1. Tap the "Leaderboard" button
2. See top 100 players by:
   ├─ Highest streak 🔥
   ├─ Most gems this month
   └─ Fastest completion time

Your Rank:
├─ Bronze (0-100 gems)
├─ Silver (100-500 gems)
├─ Gold (500-1,000 gems)
└─ Platinum (1,000+ gems)

Top Streaks:
├─ Get featured on leaderboard at 7-day streak
├─ Badges appear next to your name
├─ Bragging rights guaranteed! 👑
```

### 3️⃣ FRIENDS MODULE 👥

**What is it?**
Connect with real people via phone verification. Share your mood journey with trusted friends.

**How to Use:**

#### Connect with Friends
```

3. Search by:

```


#### Your Friends List
```
See all your friends:
├─ Their current mood

└─ Last time they were active

Benefits:
✅ See when friends are struggling
✅ Show support with messages
✅ Celebrate streaks together
✅ Challenge each other on leaderboard
```

#### Privacy & Security
```
Your phone number:
✅ Is verified during signup
✅ Is NOT shown publicly
✅ Is used only for friend search
✅ Is encrypted in our database
```

## 🤔 FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Is my data private?**
```
A: YES! 
✅ Your mood data is encrypted
✅ Your phone number is never shared
✅ Posts are anonymous (no name attached)
✅ You own your data and can export it anytime
✅ We follow GDPR & privacy regulations
```

### **Q: Can people see my real name?**
```
A: NO!
✅ Your mood posts are completely anonymous
✅ Only your username is visible
✅ Only your friends see your profile
✅ Strangers see only your mood emoji
```

### **Q: What if I miss a day?**
```
A: No problem!
✅ Your streak resets, but you can start over
✅ Completing quests tomorrow starts a new streak
✅ No penalties for missing days
✅ Mental health comes first!
```

### **Q: How do Vibe Gems work?**
```
A: Simple!
✅ Earn gems by completing quests
✅ Build streaks to multiply gem rewards
✅ Spend gems on cosmetics & features
✅ You can also watch ads for bonus gems
✅ Gems carry over forever
```

### **Q: Can I delete my account?**
```
A: Yes!
Steps:
1. Tap Settings
2. Tap "Account"
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion
5. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

Before deleting, you can:
✅ Download your mood data (CSV export)
✅ Screenshot important memories
```

### **Q: Is VibeCheck a replacement for therapy?**
```
A: NO - it's a supplement!
VibeCheck is NOT a medical app.
✅ Use it to track your mood
✅ Use it to build healthy habits
✅ Use it to feel community support
❌ DO NOT use it instead of professional help

If you're in crisis:
├─ Call 988 (US Suicide Hotline)
├─ Text "HELLO" to 741741 (Crisis Text Line)
├─ Go to nearest emergency room
└─ Call emergency services (911)
```

### **Q: How do I contact support?**
