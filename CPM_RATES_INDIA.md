## Why India CPM is Lower

### **Reason 1: Less Advertiser Competition**

```
USA market:
├─ Nike, Adidas, Apple, Google competing
├─ All bidding high prices
├─ CPM: $6+ CPM

India market:
├─ Fewer premium brands
├─ Local brands dominating
├─ Competing on budget
├─ CPM: $1-2 CPM
```

### **Reason 2: Lower User Purchasing Power**

```
USA average income: $70,000/year
├─ Users have money
├─ Advertisers pay more to reach them
├─ Each click worth more

India average income: $6,000/year
├─ Users have less purchasing power
├─ Advertisers bid lower
├─ Each click worth less
```

### **Reason 3: Larger User Base, Lower Prices**

```
India population: 1.4 billion users
├─ Massive volume of users
├─ Competition drives prices down
├─ "Quantity over quality" strategy

USA population: 330 million users
├─ Smaller user base
├─ Less competition
├─ Premium pricing
```

### **Reason 4: Ad Network Saturation**

```
India:
├─ Millions of apps competing
├─ All showing ads
├─ Oversupply of ad space
├─ Prices go down

USA:
├─ Fewer quality apps
├─ Less ad saturation
├─ Premium inventory
├─ Prices stay high
```
