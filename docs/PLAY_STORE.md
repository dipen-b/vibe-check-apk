# Play Store listing — draft copy

> Draft marketing copy for the Google Play listing. Review for brand/voice and
> the [content policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9859455)
> before publishing. Health/wellbeing claims must stay non-medical.

## App name (max 30 chars)
`VibeCheck: Mood Tracker` *(23)*

## Short description (max 80 chars)
`Log your mood in one tap. See how your city feels. Anonymous, private, kind.` *(76)*

## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
How are you, really?

VibeCheck is the small daily honest answer — 30 seconds, no audience. Tap how
you feel, and watch your week take shape. No followers, no likes, no pressure.

🫶 ONE TAP A DAY
Log your mood from six feelings, add a few words if you want, and build a streak.
Your history stays on your device.

🗺️ SEE HOW EVERYONE’S FEELING
An anonymous, aggregated heatmap shows the collective mood across the US and UK —
your city, your country, or the world. Always aggregated, never individual.

🌱 TINY ACTIONS THAT HELP
After you check in, get a quick ~2-minute activity matched to your mood — a
breath, a stretch, a small reset.

💬 TALK TO SOMEONE ON YOUR WAVELENGTH (OPTIONAL)
Opt in to an anonymous, 5-minute chat with someone feeling similarly. No names,
no photos. Messages auto-delete. A profanity filter and report button keep it
kind.

📈 INSIGHTS
Spot your patterns over the week — your best and toughest days.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No email. No phone number. No real name. No precise location. Your check-ins are
anonymous even to us. Everything is deleted after 90 days of inactivity. Built
for the US and UK with privacy regulations in mind.

VibeCheck Plus
Unlock deeper insights, 30-day history, and CSV export for $2.99 / £2.49 a
month. Cancel anytime.

VibeCheck is a wellbeing tool, not a medical device, and isn’t a substitute for
professional care. If you’re in crisis: US — call or text 988; UK — call 116 123
(Samaritans).
```

## Categorisation & forms
- Category: **Health & Fitness** (or Lifestyle)
- Tags: mood tracker, mental wellbeing, journal
- Content rating: complete the questionnaire (note: user-to-user anonymous chat)
- Target audience: **16+**
- Data Safety: declare anonymous use, **no PII**, no precise location, data
  deletable in-app and after 90 days; chat messages ephemeral
- Privacy Policy URL: host [legal/PRIVACY_POLICY.md](legal/PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
  (finalised) at a public URL and link it here

## Localisation note
Copy uses mild UK/US-neutral phrasing. The in-app UI already localises terms
(e.g. "Knackered"/"Buzzing") and currency.
