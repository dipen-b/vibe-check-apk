# How Ad Revenue Actually Works - Complete Guide

## 🎯 Simple Version (TL;DR)

```
Google shows ads in your app
    ↓
Users see ads
    ↓
Advertisers pay Google
    ↓
Google pays you 68% of what advertisers paid
    ↓
You get money
```

---

## 💰 The Money Flow (Detailed)

### **Step 1: Advertiser Bids to Show Their Ad**

```
Nike wants to show "Nike Shoes" ad to health app users
    ↓
Nike goes to Google Ads (AdWords)
    ↓
Nike sets budget: $1,000/day
    ↓
Nike pays Google: $1,000/day
```

### **Step 2: Google Auctions Ad Space**

```
Google collects bids from all advertisers
Nike: "I'll pay $2 per 1,000 impressions" (CPM)
Adidas: "I'll pay $1.50 per 1,000 impressions"
Puma: "I'll pay $1.80 per 1,000 impressions"
    ↓
Google wins the bid: Nike pays $2 (highest)
    ↓
Nike's ad shows in YOUR app
```

### **Step 3: You (Publisher) Get Paid**

```
Advertiser (Nike) pays Google: $2 per 1,000 impressions
    ↓
Google takes commission: 32% = $0.64
    ↓
You get your share: 68% = $1.36
```

## 📊 Real Numbers Example

### **Scenario: 1,000 Users, 1 Month**

**User Engagement:**
```
Total users: 1,000
```

**Revenue Calculation:**

```

```

**Per User Revenue:**
```
$459 ÷ 1,000 users = $0.459 per user per month
```

## 🔍 Key Ad Revenue Metrics Explained

### **1. CPM (Cost Per Mille/1,000 Impressions)**

```
Definition: How much advertisers pay per 1,000 ad views

Example:
- Health app: $4-6 CPM (good)
- Finance app: $8-15 CPM (excellent)
- News app: $2-4 CPM (lower)
- Gaming app: $1-3 CPM (lowest)

VibeCheck target: $5-6 CPM (health + mood tracking niche)
```

**Formula:**
```
Revenue = (Total Impressions ÷ 1,000) × CPM × 0.68
```

### **2. CPC (Cost Per Click)**

```
Definition: How much advertisers pay when user clicks ad

Example:
- Health services: $0.30-$0.60 per click
- Premium products: $0.80-$1.50 per click
- Insurance: $1.00-$3.00 per click

VibeCheck target: $0.40-$0.60 per click
```

**Formula:**
```
Revenue = (Total Clicks) × CPC × 0.68
```

### **3. CTR (Click-Through Rate)**

```
Definition: Percentage of users who click ads they see

Healthy CTR:
- Banner ads: 0.5-1% CTR
- Native ads: 3-5% CTR (higher because they blend with content)
- Interstitial ads: 2-4% CTR

VibeCheck target: 1-2% average CTR
```

**Formula:**
```
CTR = (Total Clicks ÷ Total Impressions) × 100%

Example:
1,000 impressions → 10 clicks = 1% CTR
```

### **4. RPM (Revenue Per Mille/1,000 Sessions)**

```
Definition: How much you earn per 1,000 user sessions

Calculation:
RPM = (Total Revenue ÷ Total Impressions) × 1,000

Example:
$459 revenue ÷ 135,000 impressions × 1,000 = $3.40 RPM

Healthy range: $3-10 RPM depending on app category
```

## 🎯 Google's 32% Cut - Where It Goes

```
$1.00 → Advertiser pays Google

Google takes 32% breakdown:

├─ Credit card processing (3-5%): $0.03-0.05
│  (Payment processor fees: Stripe, Square)
│
├─ Infrastructure costs (5-8%): $0.05-0.08
│  (Servers, storage, CDN for ad delivery)
│
├─ Discovery/Ad auctions (3-5%): $0.03-0.05
│  (Running massive ad marketplace)
│
├─ Compliance/Fraud (3-4%): $0.03-0.04
│  (Fraud detection, compliance, KYC)
│
├─ Developer support (2-3%): $0.02-0.03
│  (AdMob support, documentation, monitoring)
│
└─ Profit (8-10%): $0.08-0.10
   (Google's margin)

Total: 32% = $0.32
```

**This is why all platforms take 30%+** (Apple, Amazon, Microsoft all similar)

## 🔄 Payment Flow Timeline

```
Day 1-30: User sees ads in your app
    ↓
Day 31: Advertiser pays Google $675
    ↓
Day 31: Google transfers $216 (their cut) to their bank
    ↓
Day 31: Google credits your account with $459
    ↓
Day 32-60: Balance accumulates
    ↓
Month 2 End: Your balance = $918 (if $459/month)
    ↓
Day 1 of Month 3: Google pays you via wire transfer
    ↓
You receive: $918 (or $459 minimum threshold)
```

**Payment Timeline:**
```
- Earnings: Real-time in AdMob console
- Payments: Monthly (usually 21st-28th of month)
- Minimum threshold: $100 USD (they don't pay below this)
- Payment method: Wire transfer, PayPal, etc.
```

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Revenue Killers)

### **❌ Too Many Ads (Ad Fatigue)**
```
Problem: 10+ ads per session
Result: Users uninstall
Impact: Lose users → lose revenue
```

### **❌ Misleading Ads**
```
Problem: Fake/click-bait ads
Result: Google disables ads (policy violation)
Impact: Zero ad revenue = Suspended account
```

### **❌ Only Banner Ads**
```
Problem: CTR only 0.5%
Result: Low click revenue
Impact: 10× lower CTR than native ads
```

### **❌ All Impressions, No Clicks**
```
Problem: Users ignore all ads
Result: Only CPM revenue, no CPC revenue
Impact: 50% lower total revenue
```

### **❌ Wrong User Demographics**
```
Problem: All India/Africa users (low CPM)
Result: $1-2 CPM instead of $5-8
Impact: 5× lower revenue
```
