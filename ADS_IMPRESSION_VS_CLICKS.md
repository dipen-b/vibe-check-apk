## Two Types of Ad Revenue

### **1пёЏвѓЈ CPM (Impressions) - You Get Paid for SHOWING Ads**

**CPM = Cost Per Mille (per 1,000 impressions)**

```

    в†“

    в†“

    в†“
```

**The key: Click is OPTIONAL for CPM revenue** вњ…

### **Example: 1,000 Ad Views (CPM)**

```
Scenario: 1,000 users see your banner ad

CPM Rate: $5 per 1,000 impressions

Calculation:
Revenue = (Impressions Г· 1,000) Г— CPM
Revenue = (1,000 Г· 1,000) Г— $5

```

### **2пёЏвѓЈ CPC (Clicks) - You Get Paid for CLICKS**

**CPC = Cost Per Click**

```
How it works:

    в†“
User actually CLICKS the ad
    в†“
Advertiser pays Google (extra)
    в†“
You get paid (extra)
    в†“

```

**The key: Click REQUIRED for CPC revenue** вњ…

### **Example: 1,000 Ad Views with 10 Clicks (CPC)**

```
Scenario: 1,000 users see your banner ad

CPC Rate: $0.50 per click
CTR (Click-Through Rate): 1% = 10 clicks from 1,000 views

Calculation:
Revenue = Clicks Г— CPC
Revenue = 10 Г— $0.50
Revenue = $5

YOU GET: Extra $5 from the 10 clicks
```
